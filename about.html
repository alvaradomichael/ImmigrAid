The Process Of Becoming A U.S Citizen

  1. Find Out Whether You Are Eligible - To apply for U.S citizenship you need to have a green card.
  2. Overcome Barriers to Your Ineligibility.
  3. Three: File USCIS Form N-400 - File some paperwork with U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, it costs $640 to file the application for naturalization, plus an $85 biometrics fee.
  4. Get Fingerprinted - You fingerprint needs to be taken next in order to run FBI background checks.
  5. Attend a Citizenship Interview - you should receive an appointment date and address for an interview with a UCSIC officer. During this interview, the officer will go through your N-400 and confirm your answers to all the questions.
  6. Attend the Oath Ceremony - If you are accepted then you will have to attend a ceremony where you will give your oath to the United States, the be given a certificate of naturalization.
